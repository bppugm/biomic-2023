Payment procedure

If you want to participate in the conference, there are four steps that you need to follow to make a payment in the BioMIC 2023.

There are different fees that need to be paid to take part in this conference. Participants from Indonesia should pay the domestic participant fee.

Presenter
Early bird discount (until August 14) US$275
Regular US$300
Additional paper(s) US$175
Non-presenter
Early bird discount (until August 14) US$100
Regular US$150

Presenter
Early bird discount (until August 14) IDR2,750,000
Regular IDR3,000,000
Additional paper(s) IDR1,750,000
Non-presenter
Early bird discount (until August 14) IDR1,000,000
Regular IDR1,500,000

Payments cannot be made in cash; they must be via credit card or bank transfer to the BioMIC UGM account below:

Payment via credit card (USD)
For international presenter who submit their manuscript to the EDAS system. We do not recommend the local presenter to pay the conference fee via credit card because the EDAS payment currency is USD.

Payment via bank account (IDR)
For local presenter (Indonesia) can pay the conference fee to:
Account number: 9888-8002-2102-0003
Account name: BIOMIC
Bank name: BNI 46

Before confirming your payment in the confirmation form, you need to prepare these following items:

  • The scanned or screenshot of your payment proof in JPG / PDF format.
  • Your paper ID(s).
  • The attendee/presenter name.

Terms and conditions
  1. The registration fee covers seminar materials, lunch, morning and afternoon teas, attendance of all technical sessions, and publication (excluding accommodation).
  2. The registration fee covers one person only. Multiple attending authors must register separately, and those not presenting should register as non-presenters.
  3. After verifying your payment, a receipt will be generated from the payment confirmation details.
  4. Payments are nonrefundable.
  5. Payments cannot be made in cash; they must be made via e-billing or credit card.
  6. Participants are responsible for transfer fees and bank charges.
  7. At least one author for each accepted paper must register as a presenter.
  8. Participants are charged based on their institution/affiliation, not the nationality of the first author.
  9. Submitting an extra paper applies only to the same presenter.
  10. Participants can only pay the conference fees after receiving an acceptance notification email.
  11. The early bird discount will be expired on August 14, 23:59 am (GMT+7).
